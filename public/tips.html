Food Tips:
  • Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables in your diet for a range of nutrients.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Opt for whole grains for a healthier choice of carbohydrates.
  • Limit processed foods and choose fresh, whole foods whenever possible.
  • Practice portion control to maintain a balanced and healthy diet.
  • Experiment with herbs and spices to add flavor to your dishes without extra calories.
  • Include sources of lean protein, such as fish, poultry, beans, and tofu, in your meals.
  • Plan your meals ahead of time to make healthier choices and avoid impulsive eating.
